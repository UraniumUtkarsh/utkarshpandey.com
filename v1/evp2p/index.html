How is this viable?

Q: What is the 'Demand-Side' benefit?

A: Users get charging at 30-40% lower costs than public fast-chargers while avoiding the 'Infrastructure Trap' in non-metropolitan areas.

Q: How does the 'Asset-Light' model work?

A: ChargeMitra doesn't own chargers. We leverage existing private hardware, reducing our CAPEX to nearly zero and allowing rapid scaling.
